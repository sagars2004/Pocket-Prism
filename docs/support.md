  <h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>
  
  <details style="margin: 20px 0; padding: 15px; border: 1px solid #eee; border-radius: 8px;">
    <summary style="font-weight: bold; cursor: pointer; font-size: 1.1em;">Is my financial data safe?</summary>
    <p style="margin-top: 10px; color: #444;">Yes. Your data is stored locally on your device's secure storage. We do not transmit, sync, or back up your personal financial inputs to any external servers. You are the only one who has access to your data.</p>
  </details>

  <details style="margin: 20px 0; padding: 15px; border: 1px solid #eee; border-radius: 8px;">
    <summary style="font-weight: bold; cursor: pointer; font-size: 1.1em;">Why do I need to enter my state?</summary>
    <p style="margin-top: 10px; color: #444;">Finsh uses your state of residence to automatically estimate your state and federal tax obligations. This allows us to give you a "real feel" number for your take-home pay, rather than just gross salary.</p>
  </details>

  <details style="margin: 20px 0; padding: 15px; border: 1px solid #eee; border-radius: 8px;">
    <summary style="font-weight: bold; cursor: pointer; font-size: 1.1em;">Does the app work offline?</summary>
    <p style="margin-top: 10px; color: #444;">Absolutely. Since Finsh is a "local-first" app, it is fully functional without an internet connection. You can plan, edit, and view your finances anywhere.</p>
  </details>
